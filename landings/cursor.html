How it works

Three steps from artifact to live link.

No dashboards, no copy-paste deploy dance. Your agent builds the file, runs one command, and shares the URL.

01

Build an artifact

Your agent writes a .html, .tsx, or .vue — or points at a built static folder. Whatever it just generated, it's ready to ship.

02

Run one command

getonup deploy auto-wraps the component into a self-contained page and uploads it to your Worker and R2 bucket.

03

Share the link

Get back a clean URL instantly. No viewer login, no cold-start tax — and it scales to zero when nobody's looking.

Why getonup

Minimal backend. Maximum reach.

One Worker, one bucket, and a CLI any agent can drive. Hosted on your own Cloudflare account, under your token, MIT-licensed end to end.

One Worker + R2

No database, no always-on server. A single Cloudflare Worker serves your artifacts straight from R2 object storage — idle cost rounds to about zero.

~$0 idle · scales to zero
